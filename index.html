About AgreeApp AI

Smart Agriculture Platform for Monitoring, Distribution, and Food Transparency

🌱 Our Mission

To empower Indonesian farmers with digital technology, create transparent food supply chains, and reduce food waste through artificial intelligence.

🎯 Our Vision

Become the leading smart agriculture platform in Southeast Asia, connecting millions of farmers to fair markets and consumers to trusted food products.

💡 What We Do

AgreeApp AI provides AI-powered solutions for weather monitoring, distribution tracking, stock management, and product traceability. From farm to fork, we've got you covered.

🌍 Our Values

Transparency, Innovation, Sustainability, and Inclusivity. We believe technology should be accessible to all farmers, regardless of their digital literacy.
